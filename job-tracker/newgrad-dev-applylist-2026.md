# New-Grad Dev Apply List — Targeting $250–400K TC (Dec 2026 grad)

**How to read this:** You're a dev (not trading/research), graduating Dec 2026, targeting $250–400K starting TC.
Companies are grouped by *realistic* year-1 TC band so you aim correctly. TC figures are year-1 estimates
(base + bonus + annualized equity/sign-on) from levels.fyi and public reporting — they vary by team, location,
and year, and quant figures are all-cash. Verify the latest before signing.

**Strategy:** Run all bands in parallel. Quant + labs are where the target actually lives; Band D is your
safety net for offers + brand optionality. Apply the *day* roles open — quant and startups hire rolling.
For each, search the **dev** title and skip trading/research postings.

Other players: Old Mission Capital · TransMarket Group · GTS · Volant · Flow

### Hedge Funds — Multi-Manager / "Pod" Funds
Big names: Millennium · Point72 / Cubist · Citadel · Balyasny · ExodusPoint · WorldQuant
Other players: GSA · Tudor · Engineers Gate · Schonfeld · Verition · Centiva · Walleye

### Hedge Funds — Centralized / "Collaborative"
Big names: DE Shaw · Two Sigma · PDT · RenTec · Bridgewater · AQR
Other players: Squarepoint · Aquatic · Voleon · TGS · G-Research
> Caveats for a new-grad dev:
> - **DE Shaw, Two Sigma** — strong central SWE + quant-dev new-grad pipelines. Apply.
> - **RenTec (Renaissance)** — famously does *not* recruit new grads through normal channels; near-closed. Apply if
>   you want, but don't count on it.
> - **Bridgewater, AQR** — macro/slower, more research/analyst-flavored; fewer pure low-latency dev seats (AQR does
>   have real eng). Lower-priority for your specific dev+comp target.
> - **PDT, Squarepoint, Voleon, TGS, G-Research** — systematic, real eng roles; worth applying.

## Timing (Dec 2026 grad)

- **Now → fall 2026:** Quant (Band A) and big-tech (Band D) new-grad cycles open ~July–Sept. Apply immediately on open.
- **Rolling, all year:** Labs (Band B) and startups (Band C) hire continuously — don't wait for a calendar.
- **You're off-cycle (Dec grad):** You fit *both* "2026" and "2027" labeled roles. Confirm start date works; these
  firms are flexible on off-cycle starts.
- **Be interview-ready before applying** — small quant shops can run start-to-offer in ~1–2 weeks.
